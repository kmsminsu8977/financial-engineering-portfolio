## Research Categories

### 1. Stochastic Models & Market Risk

확률과정, 공분산 구조, VaR, 경로 시뮬레이션, 금리 예측을 통해 시장 리스크를 정량적으로 해석합니다.

- Research Category Page: [Stochastic Models & Market Risk](https://kmsminsu8977.github.io/financial-engineering-lab/research-categories/stochastic-models-and-market-risk)
- Projects: [covariance-risk-decomposition](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/covariance-risk-decomposition), [market-risk-var-backtesting](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/market-risk-var-backtesting), [monte-carlo-simulation](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/monte-carlo-simulation), [stock-price-path-simulation](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/stock-price-path-simulation), [interest-rate-prediction-model](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/interest-rate-prediction-model)

### 2. Derivatives Pricing & Volatility

옵션 가격결정, 몬테카를로 pricing, Greeks, 내재변동성 분석을 다룹니다.

- Research Category Page: [Derivatives Pricing & Volatility](https://kmsminsu8977.github.io/financial-engineering-lab/research-categories/derivatives-pricing-and-volatility)
- Projects: [black-scholes-model](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/black-scholes-model), [option-pricing](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/option-pricing), [greek-risk-analysis](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/greek-risk-analysis), [implied-volatility-analysis](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/implied-volatility-analysis)

### 3. Portfolio Optimization & Allocation

위험-수익률 균형, 제약조건, 리밸런싱 규칙을 고려한 자산배분 연구를 다룹니다.

- Research Category Page: [Portfolio Optimization & Allocation](https://kmsminsu8977.github.io/financial-engineering-lab/research-categories/portfolio-optimization-and-allocation)
- Projects: [optimal-portfolio](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/optimal-portfolio), [portfolio-rebalancing-strategy](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/portfolio-rebalancing-strategy)

### 4. Factor Investing

멀티팩터 포트폴리오와 Betting Against Beta 전략을 중심으로 팩터 프리미엄과 리스크 기여도를 검증합니다.

- Research Category Page: [Factor Investing](https://kmsminsu8977.github.io/financial-engineering-lab/research-categories/factor-investing)
- Projects: [multi-factor-portfolio](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/multi-factor-portfolio), [betting-against-beta-portfolio](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/betting-against-beta-portfolio)

### 5. Dynamic Portfolio Insurance

CPPI, TIPP처럼 동적 floor를 사용하는 포트폴리오 보험 전략의 방어력과 기회비용을 분석합니다.

- Research Category Page: [Dynamic Portfolio Insurance](https://kmsminsu8977.github.io/financial-engineering-lab/research-categories/dynamic-portfolio-insurance)
- Projects: [cppi-portfolio-insurance](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/cppi-portfolio-insurance), [tipp-dynamic-floor-strategy](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/tipp-dynamic-floor-strategy)

### 6. Financial Data Science

금융 빅데이터 분석과 뉴스 NLP 파이프라인을 통해 데이터 수집, 정제, 모델링, 시각화 워크플로우를 정리합니다.

- Research Category Page: [Financial Data Science](https://kmsminsu8977.github.io/financial-engineering-lab/research-categories/financial-data-science)
- Projects: [financial-big-data-analysis](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/financial-big-data-analysis), [financial-news-nlp-pipeline](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/financial-news-nlp-pipeline)

### 7. Market Signals & Event Analytics

감성 신호, CEO 트위터 이벤트, 기술적 지표를 활용한 시장 반응과 전략 성과를 분석합니다.

- Research Category Page: [Market Signals & Event Analytics](https://kmsminsu8977.github.io/financial-engineering-lab/research-categories/market-signals-and-event-analytics)
- Projects: [sentiment-trading-strategy](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/sentiment-trading-strategy), [ceo-twitter-market-impact](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/ceo-twitter-market-impact), [technical-indicator-backtest](https://github.com/kmsminsu8977/technical-indicator-backtest)
